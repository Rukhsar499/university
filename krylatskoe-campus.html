Our Krylatskoe Campus is a spectacular place to learn, discover, and achieve academic, sporting, and creative success. Easy to reach from Moscow city centre – and only minutes from popular expat areas – Krylatskoe shines impressively on the site of a former Olympic Park, which is now a protected area.

Coming into school every day, your child will be wowed by our specialist classrooms, the latest technology, and colourful communal spaces. We also have access to some of the finest sports facilities, both on and surrounding our modern campus.

ACADEMIC SUCCESS AND PERSONAL GROWTH

Our careful balance of British academic rigour, exceptional learning experiences, and high levels of personalised support inspires every student to succeed. In our enabling community, the very best native English-speaking teachers will support and challenge your child to earn fantastic (I)GCSE and A-Level results, too. Leaving our Krylatskoe Campus as well-rounded, academic high achievers, our graduates go on to study at top 100 universities around the world.

OUR SCHOOL
FACILITIES

Our Krylatskoe Campus is home to state-of-the-art facilities that motivate and energise our students. Your child will train, compete, and get moving in some of the very best sports facilities in Moscow, all just minutes away. They will seek to solve the world’s toughest puzzles in our specialist STEAM lessons, too, and embark on a musical journey in our instrumental rooms and practice suites.

  • Large gyms, a climbing wall, and Astroturf pitches, plus access to Olympic-standard facilities including a golf course, ski slopes, and ice-skating rink.
  • Dedicated music rooms and private practice suites.
  • Specialist science rooms, multimedia areas, and an open-plan library, all containing top-of-the-range interactive EdTech.
  • Dedicated Student Support Services team in place to care for students' wellness
our facilities

SPACEs where your child can FLOURISH

Every corner of our Krylatskoe Campus is modern, colourful, and inviting, ensuring our students feel inspired in everything they do.

TECHNOLOGY THAT
TRANSFORMS LEARNING

We embrace innovation at ISM, and make sure that every student benefits from our 21st century approach to learning.

The latest tech is integrated into every classroom, as our students seamlessly connect and collaborate using interactive whiteboards, iPads, and MacBooks. These tools enable high-quality STEAM learning experiences, enhanced by our exclusive collaboration with MIT, which teach our students how to innovate, be creative, and solve problems.

Being part of the Nord Anglia family gives our students access to unique technologies, too. These include our secure digital learning platform, Global Campus, where your child will study and take on challenges alongside 70,000 students worldwide.
