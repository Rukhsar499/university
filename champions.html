SENIOR SECONDARY (GRADES 11 & 12)

NIS offers a robust and diverse curriculum for senior secondary students, designed to foster academic excellence and prepare students for global challenges. This curriculum is typically tailored to support different learning styles and aspirations, ensuring students are well-equipped for higher education and beyond. Key components of the curriculum include.

  • Advanced Academic Programs
  • Global Awareness
  • Critical Thinking and Inquiry
  • Interdisciplinary Approach
  • Life Skills Development
  • Extracurricular Integration
  • Personalized Learning Opportunities

This comprehensive curriculum aims not only to meet academic requirements but also to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared to thrive in a dynamic global environment.

EXPERT GUIDANCE

EMPOWERING OUR TOMORROW:

At N.I.S, we offer expert professional guidance that will ensure your child is ready for the challenges of higher education. As well as focusing on independent study skills such as referencing, note-taking, and time management, we help our students to prepare for future-proofing by building skills and knowledge to stay relevant in an ever-changing world.

BEYOND THE CLASSROOM

ENRICHING EXPERIENCES

Throughout Secondary School, your child will take part in a variety of experiences that complement their academic studies.

From outdoor education and challenge activities to community projects, leadership opportunities, and House competitions, your child will work with others, build strong relationships, and develop a host of transferable skills.

Outstanding Experiences

A TYPICAL DAY AT N.I.S

Every day at N.I.S is packed with outstanding learning experiences. Your child will immerse in new and extraordinary opportunities, enjoy exceptional enrichment activities, and do what they love.

Knowledge feast

OOur vibrant campus is abuzz with focused learning as students delve into assignments, research, and exam preparation. Lively conversations and laughter fill the air as friendships are nurtured.

Flexi Learn

In our senior secondary school, we empower students to take ownership of their academic journey through personalized learning opportunities. Our dedicated teachers work closely with each student to craft tailored learning plans, honing in on individual strengths, interests, and learning styles. With flexible pathways and adaptive assessments, students accelerate or deepen their learning as needed. This student-centered approach not only fosters a love of learning but also equips our seniors with the autonomy, creativity, and critical thinking skills to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

Transitioning to higher education

As our students stand at the threshold of higher education, we empower them with the ultimate toolkit for success. Our comprehensive preparatory course goes beyond academics, equipping them with essential life skills. Meanwhile, our expert facilitators hone their study skills, from mastering time management to developing critical thinking and problem-solving strategies. We set our students up for a seamless transition, ready to thrive in their future academic pursuits and beyond.

Global Citizenship

In today's interconnected world, being a global citizen is no longer a choice, but a necessity. Embracing global citizenship means recognizing our responsibilities towards the planet and its inhabitants, and actively working towards creating a more just, equitable, and sustainable world. It's time to stand up, speak up, and make a difference. Through our collaboration with Rotary Club of Calcutta Universe, your child will make a real difference at N.I.S, in our community, and around the world. N.I.S is proud to mould students into global stewards.

Do what sets your soul on fire

After the final bell rings, the real fun begins! Our after-school activities offer a vibrant array of choices, from sports and games to arts and culture. Whether your child is a budding musician, a passionate artist, or a thrill-seeking adventurer, we've got the perfect outlet for your child’s energy and creativity. Join us and discover new passions, forge lasting friendships, and create unforgettable memories.
