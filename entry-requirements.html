THE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL OF MOSCOW
(ISM) ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

A thriving hub for expat families in Moscow, ISM provides an outstanding British education in a supportive and enabling international community. You can find our entry requirements here.

ISM entry requirements

We are proud to be a fully inclusive school that is open to children aged from 2 to 18. We accept applications all year round and new students can start at any time.

Application and supporting information

After filling in our enquiry form and taking a tour – either in person or virtually – you’ll need to complete your child’s online application form. You’ll also need to supply the following information:

  • Your child’s school reports from the last two years (in English).
  • The email address of a class teacher, form tutor, or Head of Year from your child’s current school, so we can request a reference.

Family interview

We will invite your family to an interview with either our Rosinka or Krylatskoe Head of Campus. This is a chance to ask questions, find out more about our school, and discuss your child’s learning needs, preferences, and goals.

English as an Additional Language (EAL)

Our fantastic EAL teachers support students with limited, little, or even no English. They organise one-to-one or small group sessions, as well as offering help in lessons. Places are limited, however, so please speak to our Admissions Team when you contact us.

Special Educational Needs (SEN)

Our dedicated SEN teams create tailored programmes for students who need additional support. This is fully personalised to each child’s needs and preferences, offering a combination of help in lessons and one-to-one sessions. Please get in touch with us to talk about how we can support your child.

Talk to our Admissions Team

Every family’s application process is unique, so we’d encourage you to get in touch with our Admissions Team to find out how it will work for you.
