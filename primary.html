CURRICULUM

One of the leading Primary Schools in Russia, the International School of Moscow’s British curriculum sets our students on the path to success. Lessons are lively and challenging, and provide opportunities for our children to think critically and become increasingly independent. Your child will also enjoy exciting creative, technological, and sporting experiences, learn languages, and take part in unforgettable outreach activities.

OUR PRIMARY SCHOOL
CURRICULUM

At our Primary School in Moscow, Russia, students aged 5-11 follow the English National Curriculum, which we offer on both our Rosinka and Krylatskoe Campuses.

Adapted to our local and international context, our British curriculum for Primary Schools provides a strong academic foundation, as well as offering diverse learning experiences that challenge, engage, and inspire. We nurture personal characteristics and values, too, ensuring every child is supported to grow socially and emotionally.

THE CORE CURRICULUM

All our students follow a core British Primary School curriculum, which develops their
knowledge and understanding in key subjects.

English language

PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

Every child is unique, and we take a tailored approach to nurturing personal wellbeing, confidence, and character. We develop strategies that encourage a productive response to adversity. And we always make sure our Primary School children can find a listening ear when they need one.

We support our children to develop a range of ‘Success Skills’, which are personal character traits they hone throughout their Primary School years. These include critical reflection, independence, creative thinking, respect, self-discipline, communication, resilience, courage, and self-care.
