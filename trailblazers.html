Trailblazers

SCHOOL GRADE PROGRAMME 3

One of the leading providers of Secondary education in Russia, our Key Stage 3 and 4 curricula blend rigorous academic teaching with outstanding care, guidance, and attention. Delving deeper into their chosen (I)GCSE curriculum subjects, your child will become a confident, knowledgeable, and driven learner. They’ll also benefit from first-class leadership, creative, and competitive experiences that enrich their learning.

MIDDLE SCHOOL FRAMEWORK (GRADES 9 & 10)

NIS provides a dynamic curriculum that prepares high school students for a world of opportunities. With a focus on an inquiry-based and student- centered approach, these ensures that learners acquire critical skills and knowledge relevant to the 21st century, key features of the curriculum include

  • Diverse Academic Program
  • Global Perspective
  • Interdisciplinary Learning
  • Comprehensive Assessment Methods
  • Extracurricular Development
  • Personalized Learning Support

This curriculum equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for academic success and lifelong learning.

YEARS 7-9

KEY STAGE 3
CURRICULUM

Our Key Stage 3 curriculum supports our students through the transition from Primary to Secondary School here in Russia. At this vital stage in their education, specialist subjects embed a deeper knowledge and understanding, preparing them for their (I)GCSEs.

Students study English, maths, sciences, geography, history, drama, art, Russian language, computing, music, and PE. Your child will also choose one other foreign language. This gives them a flavour of what it would be like to study each subject at IGCSE level, helping them to make their choices at the end of Year 9.

Our teachers will encourage your child to become a confident, independent learner. In each subject, they’ll be supported to assess their own work, identify areas for development, and establish clear goals.

Project work is an essential component of our British Secondary School curriculum, too. Students work both individually and in groups, developing important critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. Your child will also be supported to take risks, as making mistakes is a critical part of the learning process.

YEARS 10 AND 11

(I)GCSE CURRICULUM

At Key Stage 4 of Secondary School, our students study for their (I)GCSEs. Your child will learn in a way that works for them, based on their academic strengths and goals, as well as their ambitions for the future.

Our students typically study nine or ten subjects, which prepares them for the next stage of their education. This also allows plenty of time to explore interests outside of school.

We know that choosing which (I)GCSE subjects to study can be a daunting process. We will support you and your child every step of the way, exploring options, explaining how and what we teach, and identifying career and university ambitions.

Our courses are provided by the CIE and Pearson examination boards.

A TYPICAL DAY AT N.I.S

Every day at N.I.S is packed with outstanding learning experiences. Your child will immerse in new and extraordinary opportunities, enjoy exceptional enrichment activities, and do what they love.

Each day unfolds uniquely

Mindful Mondays, talk it out Tuesdays, wake- up Wednesdays, thoughtful Thursdays, and fiction Fridays-daily academic adventures get our students ready to learn, as they enjoy activities ranging from mindfulness and reading to dance and PSHE.

Thrilling discoveries

Over stream programme is packed with exhilarating experiences hands-on science experiments and practical maths challenges get young minds ticking, developing ingenious problem solvers and critical thinkers.

Eco-Explorers

Field trips at N.I.S are invaluable educational experiences that extend learning beyond the classroom walls. By immersing students in real-world environments, these trips offer hands-on learning opportunities that enhance understanding and retention of academic concepts. Whether exploring a museum, visiting a nature reserve, or touring a historical site, these excursions stimulate curiosity, encourage critical thinking, and foster a deeper appreciation for the world around us.

Sporting superstars

Our secondary students represent their house, campus, and school with pride. Training, honing their skills and keeping fit, our talented sports stars compete in basketball, table-tennis, swimming, archery, skating, chess, football, and athletics tournaments at school and across India.

Pursuing passions

From writing for the school newspaper and working towards a social cause to cooking and Zumba, our extensive after- school activity programme has something for everyone.
