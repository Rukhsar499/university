PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

Every child is unique, and we take a tailored approach to nurturing personal wellbeing, confidence, and character. We develop strategies that encourage a productive response to adversity. And we always make sure our Primary School children can find a listening ear when they need one.

We support our children to develop a range of ‘Success Skills’, which are personal character traits they hone throughout their Primary School years. These include critical reflection, independence, creative thinking, respect, self-discipline, communication, resilience, courage, and self-care.

THE SPECIALIST SUBJECTS

Alongside the core curriculum, specialist teachers, musicians, and coaches at NIS provide exceptional
musical, linguistic, and PE opportunities for our students.

Modern foreign languages

A high-quality language education fosters our students' curiosity and deepens their understanding of the world. We offer French and German to all students, from beginners to native speakers.

Music

On our extensive music programme in collaboration with Torrens and Furtado, students can learn multiple instruments, play in our ensembles, or sing in a choir. We also offer an exciting schedule of concerts and workshops, where students showcase their talents.

Physical Education

Our PE curriculum is packed with team and individual activities, including gymnastics, basketball, swimming, ice- skating, football, tennis and athletics. There are also opportunities to compete in inter-House competitions, sports days, and swimming galas.

A TYPICAL DAY AT NIS

Every day at NIS is packed with outstanding learning experiences. Your children will immerse in new
and extraordinary opportunities, enjoy exceptional enrichment activities, and do what they love.

Academic Adventure

Every morning at NIS is crammed with activities, as our primary students read, practice their spelling, catch-up with friends, share important news, and prepare for a big day of learning.

Experiential Pursuits

At NIS, we dive into the fascinating concepts of space and time, encouraging students to research and solve real-world challenges. Our enthusiastic teachers bring core subjects to life in engaging and innovative ways, nurturing masterful mathematicians, inquisitive scientists, and imaginative writers. Through this dynamic approach, we inspire a love for learning and prepare students to excel in their academic and creative endeavours.

Rhythm Rulers and Dance Dynamos

It's time to shine! Your child can learn an instrument or learn to groove to an eclectic dance form or join one of our choirs or orchestras. Our extensive music and dance programmes offer a myriad of opportunities to budding singers and dancers.

On the move

Physical activity brings so many benefits, not only getting students moving, but also developing lifelong healthy habits. NIS provides opportunities for students to build resilience, develop a sense of fair play, and learn the value of persistence and discipline.

BEYOND THE CLASSROOM

Learning never stops

The exceptional learning experiences don't stop when lessons end. After-School Activities - from Lego challenge, fashion-designing, technology, literary endeavours, coding to cooking - give your child the chance to do something fun.
