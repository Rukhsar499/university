ISM Admissions

JOINING OUR COMMUNITY

We’re delighted you’re considering the International School of Moscow (ISM) for your child’s education.

A SUPPORTIVE COMMUNITY
WHERE EVERYBODY
THRIVES

Your child will achieve more than they ever imagined possible at ISM, where our commitment to British academic excellence, breadth of learning experiences, and high levels of personalised support inspire success. This includes getting outstanding (I)GCSE and A-Level results, which consistently take our students to first-choice, top 100 universities across the world.

We’re proud to be a focal point for the international community in Moscow. Across our two stunning campuses, we provide a home-from-home to students, families, and teachers from more than 50 countries.

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

WHO CAN JOIN?

ISM is a fully inclusive international school that offers places all year round to children aged from 2 to 18. After making an enquiry, taking a tour, and speaking to our Admissions Team, you will fill in an online application form. We will then invite you and your child to an informal family interview.

STEP-BY-STEP ADMISSIONS PROCESS

Ready to start your child’s application? This is what you need to do.

Make an enquiry

Start your admissions journey today by filling in our online enquiry form.

Enquiry Now

Take a tour

The best way to get a feel for our exceptional British standards, multicultural community, and impressive learning environments is to visit us in person. We’ll happily organise a virtual meeting if you can’t make it to Moscow.

Send us your application

Your child can join us at any time during the school year. As well as filling in our online form, you’ll need to send us some supporting documents.

Speak to the Admissions Team

Family interview

After receiving your form and documents, we will invite you and your child to a family interview with our Head of Campus.

Offering your child a place

If everyone’s happy, we’ll formally offer your child a place.

Starting school

We’ll send you plenty of information – covering things like transport and uniforms – ahead of your child’s start date. On their first day, we’ll personally welcome you on to campus. We’ll introduce your child to their teachers and classmates, and show them around, too.

Academic excellence

At ISM, we work tirelessly to ensure every student fulfils their potential. At each stage of their learning journey, your child will receive the right balance of challenge and support, tailored to their needs and goals. This includes the opportunity to join our Most Able and Talented (MAT) Programme, which accelerates our most gifted students’ progress.

From the Early Years Foundation Stage through to IGCSEs and A-levels, all our students follow the world-renowned English National Curriculum. We’re the only school in Moscow to offer this rigorous British education from 2 to 18, which we carefully adapt to our international context. Used by more than 30,000 schools worldwide, the curriculum is also globally respected by the best universities.

Our personalised approach to learning – which also includes expert pastoral and wellbeing support – sees our students get excellent results. Every year, they achieve IGCSE and A-level grades far higher than the UK national average. This hard-earned success takes our graduates to the very best universities in the UK, Europe, North America, and Asia, including Oxford, Cambridge, UCLA, and Imperial College London.

OUR TUITION FEES

At ISM, our tuition fees reflect the premium education we provide, which is among the very best in Russia. From the highest British academic standards and rigorous teaching to world-class personalised care and a myriad of extra-curricular activities, we provide each student with everything they need to thrive.
