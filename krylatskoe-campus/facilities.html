JOUR FACILITIES

Learning comes to life in remarkable ways on our Krylatskoe Campus, where dynamic, interactive classrooms – as well as specialist music and language spaces – flow into bright outdoor areas and some of the best sporting facilities in Russia.

EXPLORE OUR KRYLATSKOE CAMPUS

Located on the site of the Moscow 1980 Olympic Games, our Krylatskoe Campus is an inspirational place to learn, play, and discover. Both our on-campus and surrounding sports facilities – which include ski slopes and a golf course – motivate ambitious athletes, while the latest technology powers discovery and creativity in our specialist classrooms, science rooms and library.

  • Large gyms, a bespoke climbing wall, and Astroturf pitches, plus access to an array of Olympic-standard indoor and outdoor facilities.
  • Dedicated music rooms and private practice suites.
  • Specialist science rooms, multimedia areas, and an open-plan library, all containing top-of-the-range interactive EdTech.
  • Dedicated Student Support Services team in place to care for students' wellness.

GOING FOR GOLD

Our young athletes have access to some of the finest facilities in the country, ensuring they have everything they need to achieve sporting success. Both in and outside of sports lessons, your child will take part in team games, hone their sporting skills, and do what they love. Their unique individual talents will shine brightly, too, thanks to seasonal facilities – including a water sports lake and ice-skating rink – that offer new and exciting opportunities.

IN FOCUS: KRYLATSKOE
LOWER CAMPUS

Home to our Early Years and younger Primary students, our secure Lower Campus is a colourful and inviting environment with two modern buildings connected by a multi-purpose hall, large playground area, and football pitches. Here, bright, energising classrooms – as well as specialist music, language and library rooms – flow into outdoor spaces, including a garden and allotment, which provide calming areas to connect with nature.

IN FOCUS: KRYLATSKOE
LOWER CAMPUS

Our Upper Campus is home to our older Primary (Years 5-6) and Secondary students. Here, specialist subjects come to life in state-of-the-art science rooms and tech-enriched classrooms, while large gyms, two all-weather football pitches, and an outdoor climbing wall give every child the chance to get active. Our Upper Campus also benefits from a beautifully designed, open-plan library and Sixth Form Study Centre, which is the perfect place to research, read, and revise.
