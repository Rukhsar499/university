ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

The International School of Moscow (ISM) is one of the leading private schools in Russia, providing an outstanding British education for children aged from 2 to 18. Graded ‘excellent’ in all areas by the Independent Schools Inspectorate, we pride ourselves on our students’ academic achievements, which are fuelled by high levels of personalised support, world-class teachers, and our caring multicultural community.

what we do

ISM is a hub for the international community in Moscow, providing a welcoming private school community for students and families from more than 50 countries.

Both our Rosinka and Krylatskoe campuses are secure, inspiring environments, where the world’s best native English-speaking teachers provide the personalised support each child needs to flourish.

This – combined with our exceptional British curriculum and rigorous teaching standards – lays the foundations for our students’ outstanding success, which includes 100% pass rates at IGCSE and A-level. Leaving us as well-rounded, academic high achievers, our students go on to study at top 100 universities around the world.

HISTORY AND
HERITAGE

The International School of Moscow (ISM) was founded
in 2007. We’re now home to over 700 students from more
than 50 different countries.

From day one, we have prioritised academic excellence.
We’re the only private school in Moscow where students
follow the English National Curriculum from Pre-Nursery all
the way through to A-Levels. Widely accepted as the
premier route to higher education across the world, our
globally respected British qualifications set our students up
for success, whatever they choose to do when they leave
us.

At ISM, learning takes place on two picturesque campuses.
Our Rosinka Campus welcomes children aged 2 to 11.
Located in a gated residential community in the north-west
of the city, Rosinka is a safe, family-friendly environment
that also offers an exciting outdoor programme.

Easy to reach from Moscow’s city centre, our Krylatskoe
Campus is situated on the site of the former Olympic Park,
which is now a protected area. A spectacular place to learn,
discover, and achieve academic, sporting, and creative
success, Krylatskoe welcomes students aged 3 to 18.

We joined the Nord Anglia Education family of schools in
December 2017. This has brought many benefits, including
access to world-class technology, exclusive collaborations
with Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and UNICEF, and
unique learning opportunities on our Global
Campus learning platform.

ISM is the only private school in Moscow to be graded
‘excellent’ by the International Schools Inspectorate. We are
also accredited by the Council of British International
Schools (COBIS) and University of Cambridge International
Examinations.
