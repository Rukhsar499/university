OUR EARLY YEARS
FOUNDATION STAGE (EYFS)
CURRICULUM

At our Early Years school in N.I.S, we cultivate a safe and happy pre-school environment that is the perfect place for our children to develop and grow. Our specialist teachers will get to know how your child learns best, nurturing their curiosity and interests, and guide their personal, social, and emotional growth.

(GRADE NUR-2)

Beginning The Journey of Discovery

Our Early Years & Primary Programme Adventurers introduces young minds to a world of exploration and creativity. Through engaging activities, hands-on learning, and personalised instruction, children begin their academic journey with excitement and confidence. We focus on developing foundational skills, curiosity, and social skills in a supportive environment.

The seven EYFS curriculum areas:

  • Language & Communication
  • Physical Development
  • Cognitive Skills
  • Creativity & Imagination
  • Social & Emotional Growth
  • Expressive arts and design

EARLY YEARS LEARNING
THROUGH ACTIVE PLAY

Learning at N.I.S during early years happens through play-way method. We know that children learn best when they are happy, stimulated, and engaged, which is why we offer a vast range of play-based opportunities that both captivate and challenge.

This includes lots of experiential learning beyond the classroom. Our children get hands-on experience on the concepts learnt that showcases their creativity and aids in cultivating problem- solving skills from a very young age.

A TYPICAL DAY AT ISM

Every day at N.I.S is packed with outstanding learning experiences. Your child will immerse in new and extraordinary opportunities, enjoy exceptional enrichment activities, and do what they love.

EARLY YEARS EDUCATION: GETTING CREATIVE

MUSIC AT ISM

Our students’ creativity shines, as they enjoy a range of artistic experiences. Through music, performance, and dance, our Early Years children develop their emotional intelligence, cognitive thinking, and fine and gross motor skills. Your child will sing and learn to play multiple instruments, as well as dress-up, choreograph simple dances, and perform. We encourage our students to show-off their skills in front of an audience, too, which builds confidence and self-esteem.

EARLY YEARS EDUCATION: GETTING ACTIVE

PHYSICAL ACTIVITY AT ISM

Physical activity supports our children’s all-round development, and we encourage healthy habits from a young age. Our children take part in a variety of team and individual games. A focus on fun and fitness gets our children moving both indoors and out, developing core balance and coordination skills.

BEYOND THE CLASSROOM

ANYTHING IS POSSIBLE AT ISM

At our innovative Early Years school in Moscow, your child will embrace learning beyond the classroom. From an early age, we offer meaningful activities, exciting adventures, and a myriad of clubs that will nurture their curiosity, creativity, and talents.
